Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cramberies and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet Caramelized onions, tart cramberies, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra vergin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thini sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces bries, count into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, thought it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Methods

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer With a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the ?our is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
